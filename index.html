places you must visit!

Diggins Cafe

I have visited it on December 7, 2021.

The Diggins Cafe is a small restaurant which serves continental and mediterranean food. The servings are medium, suitable for 2 people. The dishes on the menu are resonably priced and the food is absolutely delish! All the waiters actually don't even keep a notepad to note down what you order, they actually memorise it! Overall, the place is wonderful!


The Big Chill Cafe

I have visited it many times.

The Big Chill is a cafe situated in the cluster of markets in Khan Market, 110003. It has a retro ambience with friendly staff. You get large servings per dish, enough for at least 3 people. There's also a Big Chill Bakery in the restaurant, which sells absolutely scrumptious baked goodies! The service is excellent, and you walk out of the restaurant with a smile on your face, every time you visit it!


movie reviews

Secret Life of Pets 2
Delightful!

Secret Life of Pets 2 is about a cute little dog Max, who keeps on worrying about his owner's tiny toddler and refuses to let him go to preschool. Max learns to overcome his fears and worries when he goes on a trip with his doggy brother and meets a brave border collie, Rooster. The plot keeps you engaged and you laugh every two minutes while watching the film!
